about me.


I am interested in data science, machine learning & physics-informed algorithms and how they all work together. I have been very much fascinated by the diverse applications of physics-informed learning, both for forward and inverse problems, including discovering hidden physics and tackling high-dimensional problems.

To keep myself up-to-date with the newest technologies and analytic techniques, I have been attending a full-time Master's programme in Data Science and Information Technologies for the past 7 months. It has been an exciting journey, and I have learned a lot about the latest advancements in these fields.

When not studying I like to read; history and philosophy mainly. Periodically, I will try to better myself through the reading of classical literature. This happens less often now, with the addition of my girlfriend and wine into my life. Still, I am slowly making my way through the canons of Dickens, Hemingway, and Tolstoy.

I also enjoy running or swimming (often while listening to Sam Harris, Jordan Peterson or Andrew Huberman podcasts), writing (typically book reviews, or even copywork), watching the occasional movie or anime, and drinking wine.
